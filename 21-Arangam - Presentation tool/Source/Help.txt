1. INSERT A NEW SLIDE
	A new slide can be inserted anywhere in the middle of the presentation OR appended to the last slide
	Press Ctrl-M OR
	On the "Actions" toolbar, click "New Slide" OR
	On the "Insert" menu, click "New Slide"

2. APPLY BACKGROUND FOR SLIDE
	Click "Background" on the "Format" menu
	Select the options you want
	To apply the change to the current slide, click "Ok"
	To apply the change to all slides, click "Apply to All"

3. INSERT A PICTURE
	Picture can be inserted from jpg and gif files
	On the "Actions" toolbar, click "Picture" OR
	On the "Insert" menu, click "Picture"
	Locate the folder that contains the picture you want to insert
	Choose the picture file

4. INSERT A TEXTBOX
	On the "Actions" toolbar, click "TextBox" OR
	On the "Insert" menu, click "TextBox"

5. ADDING STYLES TO TEXT
	Select the text you want to change
	On the "Format" menu, click "TextBox"
	Select the options you want and Click "Ok"
	The attributes will be applied to the selected text if any, else for the text to be typed

6. INSERT A SHAPE
	On the "Actions" toolbar, click "Shape" OR
	On the "Insert" menu, point to "Shape" and choose(click) the Shape you want to insert

7. FORMATING THE SHAPE
	Select the "Shape" you want to Format
	On the "Format" menu, click "Shape"
	Select the options you want and Click "Ok"

8. DELETE A SLIDE
	Press Ctrl-D OR
	On the "Edit" menu, click "Delete Slide"

9. FIND/REPLACE
	Press Ctrl-F OR
	On the "Edit" menu, click "Find/Replace"
	Enter the word to Find/Replace in specified Textbox
	Select the options you want(Match whole word/case,Search upwards/downwards)
	Click Find/Replace

10. SLIDESHOW
	Press F5 OR
	On the "View Mode" toolbar, click "Slideshow" OR
	On the "View" menu, click "Slideshow"
	Press Esc to return to "Slide - View Mode"

11. SETTING USER INTERFACE LANGUAGE
	Press F9 - Tamil OR F8 - English OR F7 - Tamil/English OR
	On the "Language" Menu choose(click) the Language you want - Tamil OR English OR Tamil/English

12. PRINTING
	Press Ctrl-P OR
	On the "File" menu, click "Print"

13. GETTING HELP FOR ARANGAM
	Press F1 OR
	On the "Help" menu, click "Arangam Help"

14. ABOUT ARANGAM
	On the "Help" menu, click "About Arangam"
